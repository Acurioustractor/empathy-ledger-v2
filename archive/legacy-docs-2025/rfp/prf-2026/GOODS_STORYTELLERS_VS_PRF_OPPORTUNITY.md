**Purpose:**

## Part 4: The Gaps & Tensions

### Where Storytellers' Voices Reveal Areas to Strengthen

#### Gap 1: **Mental Health and Substance Abuse**

**What Storytellers Say:**
- "Alcohol and other drugs... put a lot of us in hospital" (Cliff)
- "Drinking and drugs that needs to stop" (Risilda)
- "My homeland is no alcoholic, no drugs at all" (Annie - longing for traditional way)

**GOODS Mission:** Doesn't explicitly address substance abuse or mental health

**Question for PRF Application:**
How does GOODS acknowledge that beds and washing machines alone don't solve trauma, addiction, or mental health? What's the connection between material goods and holistic wellbeing?

**Potential Response:**
GOODS creates *conditions* for wellbeing (sleep, dignity, reduced stress) but recognizes it's one piece of a larger ecosystem. The fellowship could research: *How do material improvements interact with other health interventions?*

---

#### Gap 2: **Infrastructure Beyond Goods**

**What Storytellers Say:**

Does GOODS risk being too narrow if broader infrastructure gaps aren't addressed?

**Potential Response:**
GOODS is deliberately focused (beds, washing machines, fridges) to be excellent at specific things rather than trying to solve everything. Fellowship could explore: *How does GOODS model scale to other infrastructure needs?*

#### Gap 3: **Youth Voice**

**What Storytellers Say:**
