My Recommendations

V5 Filled Arcs - Most logo-like. Solid filled shapes have the crispest edges possible. This is how major brands do it.

V3 Solid Ochre - If you want to keep the stroke style but need maximum clarity. Single color, thicker weight.

Both of these will look sharp and professional at any size, on any background.
