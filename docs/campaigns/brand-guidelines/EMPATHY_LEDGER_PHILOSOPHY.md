## üéØ **FUNDAMENTAL PRINCIPLES** (Never Compromise)

### **1. Story Sovereignty**
- **Stories belong to tellers** - forever and always  
- Every storyteller owns their content and controls access
- No orphaned content, no fabricated quotes, no extracted insights
- Every piece of data traces back to a real person who gave consent

### **2. Data Sovereignty & Community Ownership**
- **Communities control their narratives** - not institutions
- Full ownership and control over all data collected and analyzed
- Indigenous data sovereignty principles (CARE framework)
- Transparent value flow - communities benefit from their participation

### **3. Privacy by Design**
- **Privacy is fundamental** - not an afterthought
- Granular consent controls built into every feature
- Cultural protocol respect and sacred knowledge protection
- Three-tier privacy system: public, paywall, organization-specific

### **4. AI for Empowerment (Not Extraction)**
- **AI serves humanity** - not capital
- Artificial intelligence amplifies community voices
- Identifies community-defined solutions
- Never replaces human wisdom or makes decisions for communities
- Cultural sensitivity and protocol compliance built-in

### **5. Community-First Business Model**
- **Value flows to creators** - not platform extraction
- Platform revenue shared with storytellers (70/30 split)
- Major platform decisions involve community input
- Fair compensation for historically extracted wisdom

### **6. Strength-First Approach**
- Analysis focuses on community assets, innovations, and solutions
- Never deficits or problems
- Community-defined success metrics
- Empowerment measurement by community standards

### **7. Cultural Protocol Respect**
- Honor community cultural practices and seasonal restrictions
- Elder consultation requirements where appropriate
- Sacred knowledge protection boundaries
- Language and terminology preferred by communities
