## Key Principles Throughout Process

### 1. Community Voice Leads Everything

**As Dianne Stokes said: "They need to hear from the ground."**

- Storyteller quotes open every application answer
- Community priorities define research questions
- Community benefit drives decision-making
- Community veto power over all outputs

### 2. Cultural Protocols Are Non-Negotiable

- Never violate cultural protocols to meet deadline
- Follow community-specific consultation processes
- Respect sacred and restricted knowledge
- Get cultural advisor approval

### 3. Relationship > Funding

- If application would harm community relationship, don't submit
- Trust takes years to build, seconds to destroy
- This is one funding opportunity - community partnership is ongoing
- "No" from community is community self-determination in action

### 4. Evidence-Based Claims Only

- Every claim tested against storyteller evidence
- No theoretical statements without community backing
- Alfred's bed safety, Daniel's "going without," Dianne's "hear from the ground" - these are the proof
- Data and quotes support all assertions

### 5. OCAPÂ® Principles Throughout

- **Ownership:** Communities own their data and narratives
- **Control:** Communities control how information is shared
- **Access:** Communities define who can access what
- **Possession:** Communities can export data, withdraw consent anytime

## Success Metrics for Application Process

**This application process is successful if:**

1. **Community Relationships Maintained or Strengthened**
   - No storyteller feels used or disrespected
   - Trust maintained regardless of application outcome
   - Community sees benefit in conversation itself

2. **Cultural Protocols Respected**
   - All consultations followed appropriate processes
   - Cultural advisors approved approach
   - No violations of sacred knowledge

3. **Genuine Community Voice Integrated**
   - Application reflects community priorities
   - Community-defined research questions included
   - Storyteller words used respectfully

4. **High-Quality Application Submitted**
   - Meets all PRF selection criteria
   - Storyteller voices central throughout
   - Systems change potential clear

5. **Path Forward Regardless of Outcome**
   - If successful: Clear plan for fellowship implementation
   - If unsuccessful: Learnings inform next opportunity
   - Either way: Community partnership continues

**If fellowship is not awarded, that doesn't mean failure.** The community consultation process itself has value, relationships are strengthened, and learnings inform future work.
